## What is Astrio?

Most websites become outdated within a few years, yet modernizing them remains slow, expensive, and manual. Teams often spend weeks and thousands of dollars rebuilding from scratch, even when the core content and structure already exist. This creates a massive gap between how quickly the web evolves and how slowly legacy systems can keep up.

Astrio is an AI-powered modernization platform that transforms existing websites and legacy codebases into modern, production-ready web applications. Instead of starting over, businesses can import what they already have and let Astrio intelligently rebuild it into a clean, scalable, and fully editable system in minutes.

Powered by agentic orchestration, Astrio understands legacy code, plans multi-step transformations, and preserves functionality while upgrading design and architecture. It combines code translation, smart planning, and brand-aware design generation to deliver high-quality results with significantly less time, cost, and engineering effort.

### Why use Astrio
Astrio modernizes your websites and web systems faster, smarter, and more efficiently.

- **Speed** — Modernize websites and legacy systems in minutes instead of weeks.
- **Cost-effective** — Avoid expensive rebuilds and reduce engineering overhead.
- **Smart & accurate** — Preserves existing functionality while upgrading code and design.
- **Future-ready** — Generates clean, maintainable, and editable modern web applications.

### Who Astrio is for
Astrio is designed for anyone looking to modernize websites and web applications quickly, efficiently, and at scale.

**Individuals**
- Founders, entrepreneurs, and solo business owners
- Students and professionals with portfolio or personal websites

**Product and Go-to-Market Teams**

- Small and medium business (SMB) owners
- Product managers looking to modernize existing websites

**Technical Teams, Agencies, & Freelancers**

- Developers and engineering teams upgrading legacy web systems
- Agencies & freelancers modernizing multiple client websites efficiently at scale

**Enterprises**

- Teams modernizing internal tools or business-critical applications used organization-wide

### What you can modernize with Astrio
Astrio supports modernization of a wide range of legacy websites, from simple WordPress and Wix sites to complex custom builds using jQuery, PHP, and more.

- **Portfolio & landing websites** — Quickly update personal or professional sites to modern, responsive designs without starting from scratch.
- **Web applications** — Transform legacy web apps into scalable, maintainable, and production-ready platforms.
- **E-commerce websites** — Upgrade online stores with modern frameworks while preserving existing products, content, and functionality.
- **Internal tools** — Modernize enterprise dashboards and internal applications for faster performance and easier maintenance.
