## Detailed Instructions

You need more explanation? No problem.

### The Lifecycle of Legacy Projects on Astrio

Every project in Astrio follows a simple flow:

**Plan → Import → Modernize → Iterate → Publish**

### Step 1: Plan

Before you start the modernization process, you need to think what you want to modernize with Astrio and what you want to keep.

- **What is it?** (an old portfolio website, a messy blog system, or a landing page that looks like it's built in 2010)
- **Who is it for?** (yourself, your team, your customers)
- **What do you want to modernize?** (overall layout, brand color & theme, legacy code, old dependencies/plugins, etc.)
- **How should it look?** (clean, modern, playful, professional, etc.)
- **What do you want to keep?** (content, image assets, branding, etc.)

A little planning goes a long way. Even if you don't know how you want to modernize exactly, having answers to these questions helps Astrio give you better results.

### Step 2: Import

<img 
  src="/assets/images/astrio_import.png" 
  alt="Import options in Astrio" 
  className="w-full rounded-lg shadow-lg mb-6"
/>

There are several ways to import your outdated website into Astrio, depending on where it is hosted and your preferences.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Chat + URL">
    Astrio’s AI-powered chat box makes modernization easy and straighforward.
      - Provide a URL (link) of your existing website.
      - Just describe what you want to modernize in the chat box.
      - The more specific you are, the better the results.
      - Start with clear and detailed prompts.
      - You can refine and adjust your project as you go.
    
    <Note>
    Example:

    "Redesign and modernize my website, [https://ratioai.co.uk/](https://ratioai.co.uk/), for Ratio AI, an AI platform that automates legal processes for law firms, with the goal of creating a more modern, professional, and visually compelling experience that reflects a high-end legal technology brand. Maintain the existing content but significantly improve the layout, structure, and overall user experience, drawing inspiration from leading legal AI startups. Use only the brand colors #3A5E63, #F8F1EA, #FFFFFF, and #000000, and replace all current assets with newly uploaded, high-quality visuals that feel contemporary and refined. Rebuild the header and footer entirely, ensuring the header includes three navigation links (Product, Contact, and About) with a dropdown under Product that highlights “Multi-J.” On the homepage, design a strong hero section that clearly showcases the product with a compelling headline, supporting text, and prominent call-to-action buttons such as “Request a demo,” along with an integrated, easy-to-use contact form that is visually clear and conversion-focused."
    </Note>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="ZIP File">
    With Astrio, you can bring in your old website as a ZIP file.
    - Export your WordPress or any site as a ZIP on your hosted platform.
    
    OR
    
    - Manually compress all the content files (HTML, XML, CSS, JSON), assets, fonts, etc. as a ZIP.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="WordPress">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="How to import">
        Click "Import from WordPress" button from the + icon in the chat box.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add your current WordPress site URL">
        Enter a WordPress site URL, and Astrio will automatically detect whether it’s self-hosted, hosted on WordPress.com, or not a WordPress site at all.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Allow WordPress access to Astrio">
        - If the site is self-hosted, you’ll be asked to provide WordPress login details, such as your email or username and password. Astrio uses this information solely for importing your site.  
        - If the site is hosted on WordPress.com, log in using WordPress.com authentication.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select WordPress site">
        If login is successful, Astrio will show you WordPress site(s) that your account is associated with. Select a WordPress site that you want to modernize.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="GitHub Repository">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="How to import">
        Click "Import from GitHub" button from the + icon in the chat box.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Log in to GitHub">
        Log in to your GitHub account and allow access to either personal or organization account(s) or both.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select GitHub repository">
        If login is successful, Astrio will show you public and private GitHub repositories that you own. Select a repository to proceed.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select branch">
        After repository selected, Astrio will show you all the branches that your repository has. Select a branch that you want to import from.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Modernize

<img 
  src="/assets/images/astrio_plan.png" 
  alt="Astrio planning for a website modernization" 
  className="w-full rounded-lg shadow-lg mb-6"
/>

Once your system has been successfully imported, the modernization process begins. If your initial plan lacks sufficient detail, Astrio may ask follow-up questions to clarify your goals. You can also refine or create your plan by chatting with Astrio if you’re unsure where to start.

After your plan is confirmed, Astrio analyzes your existing site and extracts its **brand identity**, including colors, fonts, tone, and assets. You can review and modify these elements to better match your vision before proceeding.

Once finalized, Astrio begins modernizing your website based on your instructions and updated brand identity. Within moments, it applies UI improvements and code transformations, returning a fully modernized website preview ready for further iteration.

### Step 4: Iterate

<img 
  src="/assets/images/astrio_edit.png" 
  alt="Astrio's visual editor" 
  className="w-full rounded-lg shadow-lg mb-6"
/>

After Astrio generates your application from your initial prompt, you’ll likely want to refine and improve it. This may include adding new features, adjusting behavior or appearance, or fixing issues.

You can iterate using Astrio’s **AI chat** or the **visual editor**, depending on your workflow and preference.

For best results, make changes incrementally. Focus on one task at a time rather than attempting multiple updates at once. This helps Astrio produce more accurate and reliable outcomes.

### Step 5: Publish

<img 
  src="/assets/images/astrio_custom_domain.png" 
  alt="Astrio's custom domain integration" 
  className="w-full rounded-lg shadow-lg mb-6"
/>

Once you’re satisfied with your modernized website, you can instantly publish it using Astrio’s free hosting.

If you already have a custom domain, you can connect it to Astrio at no additional cost, allowing you to seamlessly replace your existing site with the modernized version.
