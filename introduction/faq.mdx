## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Astrio?">
    Astrio is an AI-powered modernization platform that transforms existing websites and legacy codebases into modern, production-ready web applications. 
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who is Astrio for?">
    Astrio is for anyone who wants to **modernize an existing website or web app** instead of rebuilding from scratch. Typical groups include:

    - **Individuals** — Founders, solo operators, and people with portfolio or personal sites that feel outdated
    - **Product and go-to-market teams** — SMB owners and product managers refreshing public sites customers already use
    - **Technical teams, agencies, and freelancers** — Developers upgrading legacy stacks, and agencies handling many client sites at once
    - **Enterprises** — Teams replacing or upgrading internal tools and business-critical web apps used across the company
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is Astrio different from other no-code platforms?">
    Astrio is built around **modernization**, not blank-slate builds. You bring an existing website or codebase, and Astrio helps turn it into something modern instead of making you start over.

    - **Easier upkeep** — Keep improving your site through AI chat or the visual editor whenever you need a change.
    - **Cleaner, more discoverable sites** — Outputs aim to be well structured and optimized, which helps with SEO and performance.
    - **Modern integrations** — Connect to third-party services you already use or want to add.
    - **Hosting included** — Free hosting and deployment, including the option to use your existing custom domain.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need coding experience?">
    No! Astrio is designed for users with no coding background. Our visual editor and AI assistant guide you through the entire process. However, if you do have coding experience, you'll find powerful features that let you customize beyond.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I get started?">
    Getting started is simple:
    1. **Create your account** for free at [astrio.app](https://astrio.app)
    2. **Choose your starting point** and import your outdated site—from a URL (with chat), WordPress, GitHub, or a ZIP file
    3. **Start modernization**—confirm the brand identity Astrio suggests, then let it modernize your site
    4. **Customize** using AI chat or the visual editor
    5. **Publish** when you’re satisfied on Astrio for free and add your custom domain if you already have one at no extra cost

    Many projects reach a first modernized preview in minutes; you can keep iterating from there.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free plan?">
    Yes! Astrio offers a generous free tier that includes:
    - Basic access to all features
    - Free custom domain
    - Limited credits
    - Community support
    
    Perfect for trying out the platform and modernizing a few pages of your outdated site.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take to modernize a website?">
    It depends how big and complex your site is. After you import it and confirm your plan and brand, Astrio can usually show you a modernized preview very fast. For many projects that means minutes, not days.

    When you keep improving the result, work in small steps. We recommend to fix or change one thing at a time in AI chat or the visual editor. That way Astrio can follow your instructions more reliably.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Deployment & Performance

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I publish my app?">
    When you’re happy with your modernized project:
    1. Publish instantly with Astrio’s free hosting.
    2. If you already have a custom domain, you can connect it **at no additional cost** so the new site replaces the old one seamlessly.

    Note: No server management or DevOps knowledge required for hosting through Astrio.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use my own domain?">
    Yes! After publishing, you can connect your existing custom domain to your Astrio project **at no extra cost**, so visitors see your own URL on the modernized site.

    Astrio also provides free hosting, so you don’t need to worry about any technical setup.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Astrio handle performance?">
    Built for performance from the ground up:
    - Automatic optimization of code and brand assets
    - Global CDN deployment for fast loading everywhere
    - Intelligent caching strategies
    - Edge computing for dynamic content
    - Performance monitoring and alerts
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What about scalability?">
    Your apps automatically scale to handle traffic:
    - **Auto-scaling infrastructure** handles traffic spikes
    - **Database optimization** for large datasets
    - **Caching layers** for improved performance
    - **Load balancing** across multiple servers
    - **Enterprise-grade infrastructure** for mission-critical systems
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pricing & Plans

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much does Astrio cost?">
    Astrio is **free** for everyone. If you need more credits and features, you may consider below subscriptions.
    - **Free**: Free custom domain, limited credits, basic access to all features, community support
    - **Pro ($20/month)**: Astrio badge removed, extended credit limit, accept payments, private projects
    - **Max ($50/month)**: 3x more credits than Pro, blogs with AI, admin portal, advanced web analytics & SEO
    - **Ultra ($150/month)**: 10x more credits than Pro, AI workflow automation, early access to advanced features
    - **Business ($150/month/seat)**: Real-time team collaboration, central billing and administration, priority support, administrator access and control
    - **Enterprise (custom)**: Dedicated 24/7 support, single sign-on (SSO), custom data retention controls, compliance API for observability and monitoring
    
    All plans offer free hosting (including custom domain) and deployment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    The free plan is essentially a permanent trial! You can:
    - Recieve limited credits every month
    - Modernize and publish any outdated website imported from anywhere
    - Access basic features and integrations
    - Get community support
    - Upgrade anytime for additional features
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are credits and how do they work?">
    Credits are used to modernize and continue building your website with Astrio. Each modernization and new feature requires credits based on complexity. Simple sites use fewer credits, while complex web applications with multiple pages use more.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change plans later?">
    Yes. You can upgrade anytime. Your new plan will be prorated, so you only pay the difference. If you downgrade, the change will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer refunds?">
    We generally do not offer refunds. However, exceptions may be made in certain cases. Please contact support@astrio.app for assistance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
