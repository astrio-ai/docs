## Brand panel

The Brand panel is where you define your project’s brand identity: how your site should look and feel to visitors. Astrio uses this information when generating layouts, styling components, and keeping visuals consistent.

## When you modernize an existing site

If you are modernizing a website you already have, Astrio analyzes the old site and builds a first-pass brand profile from what it finds (for example colors, typography, logos, and imagery tied to the previous design).

Before modernization runs, Astrio asks you to review and confirm that extracted brand. You can accept it as-is or adjust anything that does not match what you want going forward.

## Editing brand components

If the extracted brand is not quite right or you are setting brand from scratch, you can change the pieces directly in the Brand panel. Typical controls include:

- Name and related labels tied to how your brand is presented
- Fonts (families and styles Astrio should prefer)
- Colors (palette and key accents)
- Logos (light, dark, or context-specific marks where your product supports them)
- Images and other visual assets that represent your brand

After you save changes, Astrio uses the updated brand when continuing to modernize or iterate on your project.

## Tips

- Confirm the brand step before you rely on large layout or theme changes, so generated work aligns with your choices.
- If something still looks off after confirmation, open the Brand panel again and refine colors or logos before asking for broad redesigns in chat.
