## Astrio Payment

Astrio partners with Stripe to offer Astrio Payment. You do not integrate Stripe yourself or manage keys, webhooks, and low-level configuration. Instead, you add payments directly in your Astrio project so you can start charging for products or services with a straightforward flow with no complicated APIs, webhook endpoints, or manual setup beyond what Astrio guides you through.

## Set up Astrio Payment

If you are a project owner or editor, you can turn on Astrio Payment for that project. Viewers cannot connect or manage payments.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Astrio Payment">
    Click Connect Astrio Payment. You are sent to Stripe’s hosted flow to verify your identity and complete onboarding. Finish verification and wait until Stripe approves your account before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create products">
    After you are approved, add the products or services you sell. You can create them yourself in the product tools, or describe what you need and ask Astrio’s AI to help generate or organize products for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach products to your site">
    Link or place those products on your website in Astrio so visitors can see what you offer and choose what to buy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start selling">
    When everything is connected, you can take orders and accept payments from customers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track everything in Settings">
    Open the Payment tab in the Settings panel to see revenue, orders, customers, products, and related payment activity in one place.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Transaction fees

Depending on your Astrio subscription plan, a platform fee is applied to each transaction. The fee is added to the transaction amount and is paid to Astrio (in addition to normal card and Stripe costs where those apply).

| Plan | Transaction fee |
| :--- | :--- |
| Free | 1.00% |
| Pro | 0.50% |
| Max | 0.25% |
| Ultra | 0% |
| Business | 0% |

Fees and plan names are subject to your current billing terms in the product; use this table as the overview of how the percentage scales by tier.
