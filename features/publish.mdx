## What publishing does

Publishing turns your Astrio project into a live web app by deploying a snapshot of it to a URL you can share. Only the version you publish is what visitors see on that URL.

## Who can publish

By default, owners and editors can publish an Astrio project.

## How to publish your project

To publish, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Publish modal">
    Open your project preview and click **Publish** in the top-right corner. That opens the publish flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your public URL">
    Pick the address you want to use, or keep the generated one. Hosted sites use the **`.astrio.live`** subdomain, for example **`yourcompany.astrio.live`**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    When you are ready, click **Publish**. After deployment finishes, you will see the live URL and a copy button so you can share it quickly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After you publish

You can update the live app anytime by publishing again, change the website address (published URL) from the same flow, or move to your own custom domain for a polished, on-brand experience at no extra cost. For connecting **`yourcompany.com`**, see [Custom domain](/features/custom-domain).
